The Role of Sound Librarians

“Every voice matters — I'm here to help preserve them.”
— Jessica Jeson, Sound Librarian

At the heart of the Sound Library are dedicated librarian who connect, preserve, and amplify voices from the universal.

From organizing audio collections to facilitating storytelling events, librarian like Jesssica Jeson play a vital role in building a living archive that reflects local culture, history, and identity.
